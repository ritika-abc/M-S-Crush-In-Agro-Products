About Y7 Foods | Premium Processed Food Manufacturer & Exporter in India

Your Trusted Partner for High-Quality Processed Food Products

Y7 Foods, operating under M/S Crush In Agro Products, is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium processed food products based in Ballari, Karnataka, India. Established in 2024, we specialize in producing hygienically processed, high-quality food products that meet the growing demands of domestic and international markets.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Every product undergoes rigorous inspection and testing before reaching our customers.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized food manufacturing company known for delivering innovative, nutritious, and high-quality processed food products while promoting sustainable agricultural practices and customer satisfaction.
